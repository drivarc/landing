Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026
1. Introduction
As the Drivarc application, we value our users' privacy and data security. This Privacy Policy explains the information we collect when you use our application, how we use it, and how we protect it.
2. Collected Data
When using our application, we collect the following data: • Vehicle Information: Brand, model, year, license plate, VIN number (stored only, not used for any other purpose), color, mileage information • Maintenance Records: Maintenance type, date, mileage, cost, photos • Fuel Records: Fuel type, liters, amount, date, mileage, efficiency data • User Information: Google account information (email, name, profile photo) • Notification Information: Technical information required to send you notifications • Usage Statistics: Anonymous usage data and error reports to improve app performance • Receipt Feedback: Optionally, unrecognized receipt photos (for system development)
2.1 Cookies
We use cookies on our website to improve user experience and for analytics purposes. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. Types of Cookies We Use: Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They perform basic functions such as storing your preferences. The site cannot function properly without these cookies. Analytics Cookies: Used by Google Analytics, these cookies help us understand how visitors use the site. They collect anonymous data such as which pages are visited most, how users navigate the site. These cookies are only activated if you explicitly allow them. How to Control Your Cookie Preferences: On your first visit to our site, an information banner about our cookie usage is displayed. Through this banner, you can: • Accept all cookies • Accept only necessary cookies • Enable or disable analytics cookies from the settings section Google Analytics Cookies: When we use Google Analytics, the following cookies may be used: • _ga: Used to distinguish visitors (2 years) • _gid: Used to distinguish visitors (24 hours) • _gat: Used to limit request rate (1 minute) For more information about these cookies, please review Google's Cookie Policy.
3. Data Usage Purposes
We use the collected data for the following purposes: • Providing app functionality (vehicle tracking, maintenance reminders, fuel analysis) • Sending you notifications (maintenance reminders and important notifications) • Improving the app and fixing errors • Synchronizing your data across your devices • Improving the receipt scanning system (with receipt photos you send) • Ensuring security • Fulfilling legal obligations
4. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored in the following ways: • Cloud Storage: Your data is stored on secure cloud servers • Local Storage: Stored locally on your device for offline access • Security: All data is stored encrypted and a secure connection (HTTPS) is used • Data Segregation: Each user's data is kept separate from others • Data Retention Period: Your data is stored as long as your account is active. When you delete your account or remain inactive for 3 years, your data will be automatically deleted When you delete your account or request data deletion, your data is securely and permanently deleted.
4.1. Age Limit
The Drivarc application is designed for users aged 18 and over. Individuals under the age of 18 should not use the application. If we detect that a user under 18 has provided data, the relevant account and data will be deleted immediately.
5. User Rights
Under KVKK and GDPR, you have the following rights: • Access to Data: You can access all your collected data • Data Correction: You can correct incorrect or incomplete data • Data Deletion: You can delete all your data (Profile > Delete Data) • Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all data (Profile > Delete Account) • Data Portability: You can export your data as a PDF report (Profile > PDF Report) • Right to Object: You can object to our data processing activities
6. Third Party Services
Our application uses the following third-party services: • Google: For authentication and account management • Firebase (Google): For data storage, cloud synchronization, notification delivery, and app analytics • Google ML Kit: For receipt scanning (OCR) feature These services have their own privacy policies. Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
6.1. Premium Features and Subscription
While Drivarc offers basic features for free, it provides a Premium subscription option for advanced features: • Cloud photo storage (vehicle, receipt, and maintenance photos) • Advanced PDF reports (charts, CO2 analysis, recommendations) • Priority support • Ad-free experience Premium subscription is managed via the RevenueCat platform. Monthly and annual plans are available. You can cancel your subscription at any time. Upon cancellation, you will retain access to Premium features until the end of your current billing period. Refund policy is subject to your app store's (Google Play Store) rules. Premium subscription automatically renews. You will be charged for the next period within 24 hours before the current period ends. You can disable auto-renewal in your device's store settings.
7. Advertisements
Free users may see advertisements to support the application. Ads are served via Google AdMob. Google AdMob may use device information, usage data, and your advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can manage your ad preferences from Google's ad settings page. Premium subscribers do not see ads. Premium subscription provides an ad-free experience. Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy Google AdMob privacy policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543
8. App Permissions
The Drivarc application uses the following device permissions: • Camera Permission: Used for receipt scanning (OCR), taking vehicle photos, maintenance and expense attachments. This permission is optional; if denied, manual entry can be used. • Notification Permission: Used to send maintenance reminders and important notifications. This permission is optional and can be managed in device settings. • Storage Permission: Used to save and upload photos and documents. This permission is optional; if denied, the photo upload feature will not work. • Location Permission: Optionally used to record station location during fuel entry. This permission is completely optional and not mandatory. Location data is only collected when explicitly enabled by you. • Network Status: Used to check offline/online status and manage synchronization. All permissions are used with your notification and consent within the app. You can revoke permissions at any time from your device settings.
9. Analytics and Error Reporting
To improve our application and optimize user experience, we use the following analytics and error reporting services: • Firebase Analytics: Used to track anonymous usage statistics, screen views, feature usage, and user interactions. This data is not linked to your personal identity. • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to report app crashes and errors. Error reports contain technical information about the cause of the error and how to fix it, but do not include your personal data. Collected data is used solely for app improvements and bug fixes. It is not shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes.
10. Data Encryption and Backup
We provide multi-layered protection for your data security: • Encryption: All backup data is encrypted with AES-256 standard. This is industry-standard military-grade encryption. • Backup System: Your data is automatically backed up: - Full backup: Every 24 hours - Incremental backup: Every 15 minutes - Differential backup: Every 6 hours • Backup Retention: Full backups are kept for 30 days, incremental backups for 7 days, differential backups for 14 days. • Cloud Storage: Encrypted backups are securely stored in Firebase Storage. • Local Storage: Your data is stored in a SQLite database on your device and available for offline access. • Synchronization: Data synchronization between devices occurs over a secure connection (HTTPS).
11. Data Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach: • We will report the detected data breach to the relevant regulatory authority within 72 hours (as required by GDPR). • If the breach affects our users, we will notify you via in-app notification and/or email. • We will inform you about the nature of the breach, the types of data affected, and the measures you should take. • Data breach notifications will be made in a timely and accurate manner in accordance with our transparency principle.
12. Third Party Services
Our application uses the following third-party services: • Google: For authentication and account management • Firebase (Google): For data storage, cloud synchronization, notification delivery, app analytics, and error reporting • Google ML Kit: For receipt scanning (OCR) feature • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (free users) • RevenueCat: For Premium subscription management These services have their own privacy policies: • Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy • Firebase privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy • Google AdMob privacy policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543 • RevenueCat privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
13. Data Sharing
Your personal data is not shared with third parties except in the following circumstances: • Legal obligations: As required by court order or legal obligations • Service providers: Only to the extent necessary for service provision with the third-party services mentioned above (Google, Firebase, RevenueCat) • User consent: With your explicit consent Your data is not shared with third parties in conjunction with your personal identity for advertising purposes. Google AdMob may use its own data to personalize ads, but does not have direct access to your personal data.
14. International Data Transfer
Drivarc uses global services such as Firebase and Google. Therefore, your data may be processed and stored on servers outside your country. For international transfer of your data: • Transfer to countries with adequacy decision: Data is transferred to countries deemed to have adequate protection by the European Commission. • Appropriate safeguards: In case of transfer to countries without an adequacy decision, standard contractual clauses or other appropriate safeguards are applied. • Data minimization: Only data necessary for service provision is transferred.
15. Future Features
The following features may be added to Drivarc in the future: • Fleet Management: Corporate features for companies to manage multiple vehicles from a single dashboard • AI-Powered Fault Detection: Vehicle fault detection from voice or text symptoms • Personal Assistant: Maintenance timing and fuel savings recommendations • Location-Based Features: Find nearby fuel stations, service centers, and car washes • Business Partnerships: Integrations with service, insurance, and spare parts companies When these features are added, data collection and usage practices will be updated and users will be informed. Use of new features will be optional.
16. Cookies
We use cookies on our website to improve user experience and for analytics purposes. For detailed information, see Section 2.1.
17. Policy Changes
Changes to this Privacy Policy will be notified within the app. Users will be notified of significant changes. Policy changes take effect on the date they are published within the app.
18. Contact
If you have questions about our privacy policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us using the feedback feature within the app. Email: contact@drivarc.com
