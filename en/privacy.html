Privacy Policy

Son Güncelleme: 10 Nisan 2026
1. Introduction
As Drivarc application, we care about the privacy and data security of our users. This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect when you use our application and how we use and protect that information.
2. Data Collected
We collect the following data when using our application: • Vehicle Information: Brand, model, year, license plate, VIN number (stored only, not used for any other purpose), color, mileage information • Maintenance Records: Maintenance type, date, mileage, cost, photos • Fuel Records: Fuel type, liter, amount, date, mileage, efficiency data • User Information: Google account information (e-mail, name, profile photo) • Notification Information: Technical information required to send you notifications • Usage Statistics: Anonymous usage data and error reports for better app operation • Receipt Feedback: Optional unrecognized receipt photos (for system development)
2.1 Cookies
We use cookies on our website to improve user experience and for analysis purposes. Cookies are small text files saved on your device by your browser. Types of Cookies We Use: Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They perform basic functions such as storing your preferences. Without these cookies the site cannot function properly. Analytical Cookies: These cookies used by Google Analytics help us understand how visitors use the site. It collects anonymous data such as which pages are visited more and how users navigate the site. These cookies are only activated if you explicitly consent. How Can You Control Your Cookie Preferences? When you first visit our site, an information banner about our use of cookies will be displayed. Via this banner: • You can accept all cookies • You can only accept essential cookies • You can turn analytical cookies on or off in the settings section You can change your preferences at any time. Your cookie preferences are stored in your browser's local storage. Google Analytics Cookies: When we use Google Analytics, the following cookies may be used: • _ga: Used to distinguish visitors (2 years) • _gid: Used to distinguish visitors (24 hours) • _gat: Used to limit the request rate (1 minute) For more information about these cookies, you can review Google's Cookie Policy. 3. Data Usage Purposes
We use the data we collect for the following purposes: • Providing application functionality (vehicle tracking, maintenance reminders, fuel analysis) • Send you notifications (maintenance reminders and important notifications) • Improving the application and fixing bugs • Synchronize your data between your devices • Improving the receipt reading system (with the receipt photos you send) • Provide security • Fulfilling legal obligations
4. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored in the following ways: • Cloud Storage: Your data is stored on secure cloud servers • Local Storage: Stored locally on your device for offline access • Security: All data is stored encrypted and secure connection (HTTPS) is used • Data Segregation: Each user's data is kept separate from each other • Data Retention Period: Your data is stored as long as your account is active. Your data will be automatically deleted when you delete your account or are inactive for 3 years When you delete your account or request data deletion, your data will be securely deleted permanently.
4.1. Age limit
The Drivarc application is designed for users aged 18 and over. Individuals under the age of 18 should not use the application. If we detect that a user under the age of 18 has collected data, the relevant account and data will be deleted immediately.
5. User Rights
You have the following rights under KVKK and GDPR: • Access to Your Data: You can access all your collected data • Data Correction: You can correct incorrect or missing data • Delete Data: You can delete all your data (Profile > Delete Data) • Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all your data (Profile > Delete Account) • Data Portability: You can export your data as a PDF report (Profile > PDF Report) • Right to Object: You can object to our data processing activities
6. Third Party Services
Our app uses the following third-party services: • Google: For authentication and account management • Firebase (Google): For data storage, cloud sync, push notifications and app analytics • Google ML Kit: For receipt reading (OCR) feature These services have their own privacy policies. Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
6.1. Premium Features and Subscription
While the Drivarc app offers basic features for free, it offers a Premium subscription option for advanced features: • Cloud photo storage (vehicle, receipt and maintenance photos) • Advanced PDF reports (graphs, CO2 analysis, recommendations) • Priority support • Ad-free experience Premium subscription is managed through the RevenueCat platform. Monthly and annual plans are available. You can cancel your subscription at any time. In case of cancellation, you will continue to have access to Premium features until the end of the current payment period. The return policy is subject to the rules of your affiliated app store (Google Play Store). Premium subscription automatically renews. The next period fee will be charged to your account 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can turn off auto-renewal in your device's store settings.
7. Advertisements
Free users may see ads to support the app. Ads are served through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may use device information, usage data and your advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can manage your ad preferences on Google's ad settings page. Premium subscribers do not see ads. Premium subscription offers an ad-free experience. Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy Google AdMob privacy policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543
8. App Permissions
The Drivarc app uses the following device permissions: • Camera Permit: Used for receipt scanning (OCR), vehicle photography, maintenance and expense attachments. This permission is optional and if denied, manual entry can be made. • Notification Permission: Used to send maintenance reminders and important notifications. This permission is optional and can be managed in device settings. • Storage Permission: Used to save and upload photos and documents. This permission is optional and if denied the photo upload feature will not be available. • Location Permission: Optionally can be used to record station location during fuel recording. This permission is completely optional and not mandatory. Location data is collected only when enabled by you. • Network Status: Used to check offline/online status and manage synchronization. All permissions are used by informing you within the application and obtaining your approval. You can revoke permissions at any time from device settings.
9. Analytics and Error Reporting
We use the following analytics and error reporting services to improve our application and optimize user experience: • Firebase Analytics: Used to track anonymous usage statistics, screen views, feature usage, and user interactions. This data is not associated with your personal identity. • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to report application crashes and errors. Error reports contain technical information about the cause of the error and its correction, but do not include your personal data. The data collected is used only for application improvements and bug fixes. It is not shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes.
10. Data Encryption and Backup
We provide multi-layered protection for the security of your data: • Encryption: All backup data is encrypted with the AES-256 standard. This is industry standard military-grade encryption. • Backup System: Your data is automatically backed up: - Full backup: Every 24 hours - Incremental backup: Every 15 minutes - Differential backup: Every 6 hours • Backup Retention: Full backups are retained for 30 days, incremental backups are retained for 7 days, and differential backups are retained for 14 days. • Cloud Storage: Your encrypted backups are securely stored in Firebase Storage. • Local Storage: Your data is stored in a SQLite database on your device and is available for offline access. • Synchronization: Data synchronization between devices is performed via secure connection (HTTPS).
11. Data Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach: • We will report the detected data breach to the relevant regulatory authority within 72 hours (as required by the GDPR). • If the breach affects our users, we will notify you via in-app notification and/or email. • We will inform you about the nature of the breach, the types of data affected and the measures that need to be taken. • Data breach notifications will be made in a timely and accurate manner in accordance with our principle of transparency.
12. Third Party Services
Our app uses the following third-party services: • Google: For authentication and account management • Firebase (Google): For data storage, cloud sync, push notifications, app analytics and error reporting • Google ML Kit: For receipt reading (OCR) feature • Google AdMob: For ad display (free users) • RevenueCat: For premium subscription management These services have their own privacy policies: • Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy • Firebase privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy • Google AdMob privacy policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543 • RevenueCat privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
13. Data Sharing
Your personal data is not shared with third parties except in the following cases: • Legal obligations: Due to court decision or legal obligations • Service providers: Only to the extent necessary for service provision with the above-mentioned third-party services (Google, Firebase, RevenueCat). • User consent: In accordance with your express consent Your data is not associated with your personal identity and shared with third parties for advertising purposes. Google AdMob may use its own data to personalize ads, but it does not have direct access to your personal data.
14. International Data Transfer
Drivarc application uses global services such as Firebase and Google. Therefore, your data may be processed and stored on servers outside Turkey. In case of international transfer of your data: • Transfer to countries with adequate protection orders: Data is transferred to countries with adequate protection orders by the European Commission. • Appropriate safeguards: In case of transfer to countries without adequate safeguards, standard contractual clauses or other appropriate safeguards are applied. • Data minimization: Only data necessary for service delivery is transferred.
15. Future Features
The following features may be added to the Drivarc app in the future: • Fleet Management: Enterprise features for companies to manage multiple vehicles from a single panel • Artificial Intelligence Supported Malfunction Prediction: Vehicle malfunction prediction from audio or text symptoms • Personal Assistant: Maintenance time and fuel saving recommendations • Location-Based Features: Finding nearby gas stations, shuttles and car washes • Business Partnerships: Integrations with service, insurance and spare parts companies When these features are added, data collection and usage practices will be updated and users will be notified. Use of new features will be optional.
16. Cookies
We use cookies on our website to improve user experience and for analysis purposes. For detailed information, see Section 2.1.
17. Policy Changes
Changes to this Privacy Policy will be notified within the application. Notifications will be sent to our users for important changes. Policy changes take effect on the date they are published within the application.
18. Communication
If you have questions about our privacy policy or would like to exercise your data rights, please contact us using the feedback feature within the app. Email: contact@drivarc.com
